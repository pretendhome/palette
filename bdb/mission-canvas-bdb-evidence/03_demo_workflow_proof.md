# Evidence 3 - BDB Demo Workflow

## Demo sequence

1. PROTECT - client-specific strategy question
   - Example: What is our exposure if the majority member at Meridian Holdings was self-dealing on the .3M contract?
   - Expected route: LOCAL
   - Proof point: external research is blocked; zero client facts leave the machine.

2. RESEARCH - public-law question
   - Example: What Delaware case law applies to majority member self-dealing?
   - Expected route: governed external research via Perplexity
   - Proof point: Perplexity acts as the external research layer, controlled by Mission Canvas.

3. DECIDE - different intent, same governance layer
   - Example: How should a founder decide whether this is a one-way or two-way door?
   - Proof point: same visible pipeline, different model/intent behavior.

## Closing line

Four AI models worked the matter. None of them know the client exists. The tools come and go. The judgment stays.

## Product impact
The demo shows the full Mission Canvas loop: classify, govern, route, synthesize, and store judgment.
