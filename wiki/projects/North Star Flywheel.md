# North Star Flywheel

The virtuous cycle connecting Palette (knowledge engine), Mission Canvas (decision surface), and Enablement (learning delivery). Every implementation feeds back into the system.

## The Cycle

1. **Palette** produces knowledge entries, taxonomies, and routing intelligence
2. **Enablement** applies that knowledge to real learners and real problems
3. **Implementations** generate evidence, patterns, and feedback
4. **Palette** absorbs the learnings, growing the knowledge base
