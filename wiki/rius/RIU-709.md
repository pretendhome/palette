# Fiduciary Duty Analysis

Action by a board, officer, trustee, or fiduciary requires analysis against applicable duty standards.

## Definition

Action by a board, officer, trustee, or fiduciary requires analysis against applicable duty standards.

**Execution approach:**

Identify the fiduciary role (director, officer, trustee, partner, adviser). Determine applicable standard of care (business judgment, enhanced scrutiny, entire fairness). Map to relevant precedents by jurisdiction. Assess whether the proposed action meets the applicable standard. Flag enhanced scrutiny triggers (conflict, sale-of-company, controller transaction). Public precedent research can go external; specific client analysis stays local.
