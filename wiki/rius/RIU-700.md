# Privilege Risk Assessment

A query or action may expose attorney-client privileged material to a third party, including cloud AI tools.

## Definition

A query or action may expose attorney-client privileged material to a third party, including cloud AI tools.

**Execution approach:**

Classify the query for privilege risk. Identify privileged elements (client identity, legal strategy, matter-specific advice). If privileged content is detected, block external routing and respond local-only. Log the gate decision with matter reference. Reference: United States v. Heppner (SDNY 2026) — cloud AI use waives privilege.
