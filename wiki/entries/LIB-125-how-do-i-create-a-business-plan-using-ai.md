# How do I create a business plan using AI research and presentation tools?

Combine AI research (Perplexity for market data, competitive landscape, and TAM/SAM/SOM estimates) with AI presentation tools (Gamma for polished decks). Palette's Narrator agent produces the narrative and evidence base; Gamma's API converts that into investor-ready presentations. Structure — executive summary, problem statement, market analysis (Perplexity-sourced with citations), solution, business model, competitive positioning, financial projections, and team. Key decision — use Perplexity for ALL market claims so every number has a source URL. Use Gamma for the visual layer so non-designers can produce professional output. The business plan is a Palette deliverable that routes through Narrator to Gamma pipeline.
