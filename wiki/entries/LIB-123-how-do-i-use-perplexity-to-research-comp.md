# How do I use Perplexity to research comparable organizations for strategic positioning?

Use Perplexity API or MCP integration for comparable organization research. Structure queries as specific comparisons rather than broad searches — ask for companies with similar tech stack, funding stage, team size, and target market. Perplexity returns sourced, cited answers which makes it superior to raw web search for Researcher's research workflow. Cross-reference Perplexity results with Crunchbase for funding validation and LinkedIn for team composition. For competitive landscape scans, use Perplexity's recency advantage — it indexes recent funding rounds, product launches, and hiring patterns that static databases miss. Always verify funding figures from at least two sources before including in deliverables.
