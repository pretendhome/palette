The most important comment (emailhuynhhuy): "The shift for me was from second brain to something closer to a decision system." That is exactly what Convergence Board does.

My recommendation: Ship Convergence Board as proposed (Option A). Add vault health metrics to the overview modal (orphan notes, stale notes, convergence distribution) as a nod to the wiki maintenance pattern. The application essay ties it together: structured decision convergence is the missing layer in the LLM Wiki pattern.

The pitch: Every wiki tool optimizes for accumulation. We optimize for resolution.
