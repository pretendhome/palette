== WHAT GEMINI GOT WRONG ==

- Risk register is too small. 5 risks for a client engagement is not enough.
- Did not reference the existing Mission Canvas codebase in the ADR. The workspace IS Mission Canvas — the ADR should say so explicitly.

== THE SYNTHESIS ==

What happened in the last 2 hours is that the team collectively designed a product. Not an architecture. Not a feature list. A product.

Gemini started the conversation (credit for initiative). But the product emerged from the crew:
- Codex defined WHAT the product is (decision-and-artifact engine)
