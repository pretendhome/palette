The Problem

You have 5 AI agents. None of them talk to each other.

Every knowledge worker will use 3-5 AI tools within two years. No one has built the layer that sits above all of them — routing queries to the right model, grounding responses in verified knowledge, governing what agents are allowed to do, and giving voice to the whole workflow.

Without orchestration, agents act alone. Without governance, they act unchecked. Without grounding, they hallucinate. Without voice, they're just text boxes.

What We Built

Palette: a governed runtime for multi-agent AI.

You talk to it. It coordinates a team of agents — each with its own voice, memory, and behavioral rules — grounded in a verified knowledge library, controlled by auditable governance. The system doesn't just make you more efficient. It makes you more capable.
